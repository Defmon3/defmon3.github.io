Hi, I am Defmon3.

I am an independent OSINT researcher. My mission is to document and counter the activities of the Russian Federation using open sources.

I run this project in my spare time alongside a full-time job.

I design and maintain investigative tools.

I collect and verify data at scale.

I publish results others can act on.

I host a community Discord server for coordination and support and operate Aleph for searchable datasets and documents.

The project relies on paid services including Google Cloud for hosting websites, databases, and tools; Scribble Maps for mapping and collaboration; and commercial satellite services for imagery analysis.

Current focus areas

  • Tracking Russian military units
  • Monitoring Russian social media accounts
  • Documenting Russian personnel losses
  • Mapping infrastructure, industry, and personnel networks

Outputs include datasets, trackers, dashboards, concise reports, and APIs designed for clarity, reuse, and accountability.
